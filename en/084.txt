Christmas and the year 2010 are just around the corner. While most of
you are busy with the final Christmas preparations, statesmen in
Copenhagen are debating how to avert the climate catastrophe, and the
struggle over healthcare reform continues in the American Senate.

TODO

The all-important question is whether the Senate will manage to vote
on the bill before the Christmas break, thereby bringing us all a
step closer to a national health insurance. Because even if the
Senate votes and the senators pass the reform, the historic step of a
health insurance for all is far from complete, as the whole thing
then has to go back to the House of Representatives and be reconciled
with the version of the reform passed there.

The mills grind slowly. Yet we really need the reform urgently
because the US healthcare system is seriously ailing. So that you can
check if you know the facts, I have put together a small quiz for you
below.

In this spirit, we wish our loyal newsletter readers a Merry
Christmas and a healthy New Year!
